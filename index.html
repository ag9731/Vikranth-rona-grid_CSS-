Vikranth Rona

Vikrant Rona is an upcoming Indian Kannada-language fantasy action-adventure film directed by Anup Bhandari.[7][8] It stars Sudeep in the title role alongside Nirup Bhandari, Neetha Ashok, and Jacqueline Fernandez.[9] The film is scheduled to be released theatrically worldwide on 28 July 2022.[10]
